# Tempo

Tempo is a privacy-conscious job application autofill assistant from MSTapps.

It helps you keep resume, CV, cover letter, work history, education, skills, and reference details in your browser, then fill repetitive job application fields after you click the extension.

## What Tempo Does

- Stores your job application profile locally in your browser
- Lets you preview detected fields before filling
- Fills empty visible job application fields after you click
- Imports text, Markdown, HTML, and JSON resume/profile files locally
- Scans resume-like pages after you request it
- Supports common employer, government, and education application form patterns

## Privacy

Tempo is local-first. MSTapps does not collect, sell, transmit, or share your profile data.

Tempo does not submit applications, answer eligibility questions, bypass CAPTCHA, or fill sensitive fields such as passwords, SSNs, tax IDs, birth dates, or payment details.
