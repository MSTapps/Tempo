Tempo Privacy Policy

Publisher: MSTapps. Support: TempoSupportApp@gmail.com.

Summary

This extension stores resume, CV, cover letter, work history, education, reference, and related job application profile information locally in your browser. It does not sell, transmit, or share this information.

Data Stored Locally

The extension may store the information you enter or import, including name, email, phone number, address, employment history, education history, references, skills, summaries, and cover letters. Uploaded text, HTML, Markdown, or JSON documents may be parsed locally to help fill your profile.

Page Access

The extension does not run on every page automatically. It uses active-tab access only after you click an extension action such as preview, autofill, or scan resume-like page. It does not submit job applications.

Data Sharing

The extension does not transmit profile data to external servers. Autofill data is inserted only into the page you choose to fill.

Analytics and Remote Services

Tempo does not use analytics, telemetry, remote logging, crash reporting, remote AI processing, sync services, or cloud backup. Resume parsing features run locally for supported text-based files.

Sensitive Fields

Tempo is designed to skip sensitive fields such as passwords, Social Security numbers, tax IDs, birth dates, payment card details, bank account details, and routing numbers.
